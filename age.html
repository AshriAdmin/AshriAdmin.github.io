Ashri GeoExperts

Ashri GeoExperts (AGE) is a professional enterprise that offers consultation, training, and geo-services to both national and international organizations. Its mission is to develop and implement creative solutions to work out challenges and strengthen professionals’ knowledge in the fields of oil, gas, water, environment, and mining. AGE is backed by its multidisciplinary associates’ cumulative experience of 50+ years. AGE fellows have provided technical services to the following Clients:
